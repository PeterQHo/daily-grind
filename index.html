The Goal of this project is to show a different drink Special for each day of the week. This is accomplished by defining the number of the week which is from 0-6. Depending on the day of the week the daily special for the store will be different.

Since there are seven days there will be seven different drink specials. This will also mean that not only will the drink change, but the page will change 7 different times. Depending on the day the page will change the image of the drink the background of the page and the description of the drink.
